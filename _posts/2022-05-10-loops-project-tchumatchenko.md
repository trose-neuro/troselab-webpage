We are pleased that our collaborative project with **Tatjana Tchumatchenko** is supported in the context of the DFG Priority Programme **SPP 2411 “Sensing LOOPS”**. See the DFG announcement [**info wissenschaft 22-35**](https://www.dfg.de/en/news/news-topics/announcements-proposals/2022/info-wissenschaft-22-35), published on **May 10, 2022**.

This support strengthens our joint work on cortico-subcortical interactions and adaptive sensing.
